Snooker Self-Trainer

Overview

Snooker Self-Trainer is your all-in-one snooker practice assistant. Whether you're a beginner or seasoned player, this app helps you build skills, stay consistent, and track real progress—just like a fitness tracker, but for snooker.

Who's It For?

  • Beginners seeking a guided path into snooker
  • Intermediate players wanting structure and consistency
  • Advanced players tracking performance over time
  • Coaches and serious hobbyists aiming for measurable improvement

Why Snooker Self-Trainer?

  • Purpose-built for cue sport training
  • Simple, intuitive UI with no clutter
  • Works offline — train anywhere
  • No ads or distractions, just pure practice

Download Snooker Self-Trainer today and start turning your practice into real performance.

Key Features

  • Structured Drill Training: Choose from a rich library of drills tailored to every aspect of your game—potting, cue control, safety play, break building, and more. Track your sets, reps, and success rates.
  • Personalized Training Plans: Build your own weekly schedule or follow auto-generated plans based on your skill level and focus areas. Plan smart, rest wisely, and train with intention.
  • Self-Assessment Exams: Test your skills with customizable snooker exams. Record results shot by shot and get detailed breakdowns of your performance.
  • Dashboard & Stats: Stay on top of your progress with weekly reports, plan adherence tracking, pot time, and personalized drill recommendations.
  • Interactive Snooker Handbook: Learn everything from basics to advanced strategy in a structured, offline-accessible knowledge base. A built-in AI coach is coming soon!
  • Track Your Games: Log your competitive or casual games, record scores, and monitor trends in form, shot quality, break building, and positional play.
  • Powerful History & Progress Tracking: Review your entire training journey via a calendar view, drill stats, activity charts, and performance summaries.
