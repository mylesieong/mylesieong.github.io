Pool & Billiards Self-Trainer

Overview

Whether you're a beginner learning cue basics or a seasoned player refining advanced techniques, Pool & Billiards Self-Trainer is your all-in-one training assistant for 8-ball, 9-ball, straight pool, and beyond.

Built For Serious Players:

  • Works offline — train anytime, anywhere
  • Clean, modern interface using the latest Compose UI
  • Built-in calendar, stat tracking, and future AI integration

Why Choose Pool & Billiards Self-Trainer?

Because casual practice isn't enough. Get structure, insight, and real improvement.

Ideal for:

  • Pool, Billiards, and carom players
  • Billiards coaches and trainers
  • Amateur league players and solo learners
  • Cue sport enthusiasts of all levels

Get started today and bring structure to your practice! Train smarter, shoot sharper — with Pool & Billiards Self-Trainer.

Key Features

  • Drill Practice: Practice-focused drill library with filters for difficulty, time, and skill type. Set-based structure like a fitness app – track reps, notes, and success. Save and review every session.
  • Personalized Training Plans: Build custom plans based on your level, schedule, and skill goals. Automatic progression and rest days. Daily reminders to stay on track.
  • Game & Match Analysis: Log game performance: wins/losses, break stats, positional play, and more. Visualize your growth with match-by-match stats and trends.
  • Knowledge Base + Interactive Handbook: Learn rules, techniques, and strategies in a structured format. Offline access for anytime reference. (Coming Soon) "Ask AI Assistant" for on-the-spot answers.
  • Self-Assessment Exams: Simulate real scenarios with exam-style drills. Track accuracy, consistency, and areas for improvement.
  • Progress Tracking & History: View all activity in a calendar. Monitor weekly hours, drills completed, and key stats over time.
