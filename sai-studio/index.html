Frequently Asked Questions

Strategic / Legal

Can I buy back the 5% later?

Yes. I offer a Buyback Clause (Repurchase Option) for future funding rounds. Your company can reclaim the equity under specific conditions — I’ll share details in our 1:1 chat.

Who owns the Intellectual Property (IP)?

You do. Per the EASE framework, all IP developed during the service period is assigned to your company.

What happens during fundraising?

Clean Cap Table: You own the IP and maintain control. The EASE agreement is a Founder Institute standard recognized by VCs.

What if I bring in a technical co-founder?

No conflict. You own the code. Any developer can continue work — the repo is yours. My equity stays in place unless you exercise the buyback option.

What if we exit before 12 months?

The refund guarantee applies to ARR within 12 months. If you exit (acquisition, etc.) before then, terms depend on the deal structure — we’ll cover this in the 1:1.

Is the 5% equity negotiable?

The standard is 5% via EASE. Larger builds may adjust scope; the equity percentage stays fixed to keep alignment clear.

Delivery & Scope

What defines the scope of MVP?

Core user journeys plus an essential support feature that fits your needs. I use Material 3 for UI. For Beta, I add a full design phase and curated feature set.

What if my idea is complex?

I ship the smallest viable version in 1 week to get you to market. Scope is not carried on after MVP unless you choose to move up to the next package. Then I expand in phases based on real user feedback.

What's not included in 7 days?

Beyond core features: heavy custom design, third-party integrations, complex backend logic. I phase these post-launch.

Is there a maintenance period?

MVP Build has no maintenance period — it includes an Acceptance Test Run for you to sign off before acceptance. MVP to Published has a negotiable maintenance period. After that, I offer ongoing development at transparent market rates.

How are new features priced?

I estimate complexity and quote per feature. Rates are transparent and at market level — you keep building with someone who already knows your codebase.

Financial

What counts as ARR?

Recurring contract value or subscriptions — including annual B2B contracts paid upfront (prorated to ARR). Stripe, revenue dashboards, or similar. One-time grants or non-recurring sales don’t count toward the 20× trigger.

What if revenue is disputed?

We verify via Stripe or your revenue dashboard. I’ll share the exact verification process in our 1:1.

What if we pivot before generating revenue?

Pivot-friendly. Extending the product = phased build. New product from scratch = new MVP package (cash only). Total equity always capped at 5%.
