Bible Project Case Study

Motivation / Problem

Existing Bible reader apps have poor UX and lack verse explanations. We aimed to create a better experience for new users.

Team & Roles

  • You (developer) — AI integration & development
  • Partner 1 — UI design
  • Partner 2 — Product & Marketing

Timeline / Process

  • Week 1: MVP with basic Bible reader; UI and product teams parallel work
  • Week 2: UI improvements, AI-powered verse explanations; beta product ready
  • Week 3: Publish app on App Store / Play Store; polish & bug fixes
  • Week 4: Marketing campaigns, ad integration, pricing, analytics, and user reviews

Outcome

  • Published, usable app with innovative features
  • Smooth collaboration and time-limited weekly workflow ensured focus on key goals
