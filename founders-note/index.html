The problem

The hardest part of building a startup isn’t motivation.

It’s knowing what to focus on next.

💡

Too many ideas. No clear direction.

Founders spend endless hours researching, brainstorming, and second-guessing instead of building.

⚡

Energy gets drained on the wrong things.

Constant switching between planning, notes, tasks, and tools kills momentum fast.

🧭

No experienced guidance when it matters.

Important calls often happen alone, with no one who knows your full story in the room.

The workspace

Notes, tasks, calendar, then your agents.

Privacy & security

Encrypted by default. Readable by you.

In the beta, your notes, tasks, calendar, and agent threads are encrypted in transit and at rest. We can’t read your workspace content. Other users can’t either.
